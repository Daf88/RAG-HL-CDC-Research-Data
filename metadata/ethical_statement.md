# Ethical & Data Handling Statement

## Data Provenance and Anonymization
This repository contains synthetic or fully anonymized data generated exclusively for methodological reproducibility research. No real personal, medical, financial, or organizational identifiers are included in any dataset. All case study structures preserve logical fidelity to real-world scenarios while ensuring complete compliance with:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation, EU 2016/679)
- Moroccan Law 09-08 on personal data protection
- French Data Protection Act (Loi Informatique et Libertés)

## External Knowledge Sources
External regulatory and clinical guidelines referenced by the RAG module are cited by their official publication URLs and accessed between January–April 2026. These include:

1. **Healthcare**: HAS (French National Authority for Health), *Therapeutic Strategy for Patients Living with Type 2 Diabetes*, May 2024.  
   URL: https://www.has-sante.fr/upload/docs/application/pdf/2024-06/strategie_therapeutique_du_patient_vivant_avec_un_diabete_de_type_2_-_recommandations.pdf

2. **Finance (Fraud Prevention)**: Banque de France, *Observatory for the Security of Payment Methods – Annual Report 2024*.  
   URL: https://www.banque-france.fr/system/files/2025-09/OSMP-2024_VF.pdf

3. **Recruitment**: French Labor Code, Article L. 1142-1 (Updated 2026-04-11).  
   URL: https://codes.droit.org/PDF/Code%20du%20travail.pdf

4. **Education**: Moroccan Ministry of National Education, *Memorandum No. 087-25 concerning candidacy for promotion by selection for the year 2024*, September 22, 2025.  
   URL: https://www.men.gov.ma/sites/default/files/publication_docs/118-N08725220925.pdf

All sources are publicly accessible institutional documents. Excerpts reproduced for methodological illustration fall under academic fair use and do not exceed 10% of original document length.

## Human-in-the-Loop Simulation
Human intervention logs simulate expert routing decisions based on publicly available institutional guidelines. No real clinicians, HR officers, fraud analysts, central bankers, or education administrators were involved in data generation. Expert roles (endocrinologist, fraud analyst, HR recruiter, central HR officer) and their decisions are simulated to demonstrate the RAG-HL/CDC framework's methodological workflow.

## Reproducibility and Transparency
All synthetic cases, prompt evolution history, and anonymized intervention logs are provided to enable independent verification of the framework's claims. Researchers are encouraged to replicate the methodology using their own domain-specific knowledge sources while adhering to the same ethical principles of data minimization, purpose limitation, and transparency.
